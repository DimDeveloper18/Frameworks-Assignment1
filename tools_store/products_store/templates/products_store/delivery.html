Delivery Information

Thank you for shopping with us! Below are the details regarding our delivery services. We deliver our products across the country and to the Europe. All our orders are processed within 2 business days.

International shipping to the EU is estimated to take 7 business days or more, depending on the location.

Tracking Info:

Once your order has been shipped, you will be provided with a tracking number by email, which will allow you to monitor the status of your shipment.

Handling and safety procedures:

We take extra care when packaging our power tools in order to ensure their safe arrival. Upon receiving your package, please inspect it carefully for any signs of damage and contact us immediately if you notice any.
